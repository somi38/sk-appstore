# Reactive Resume
Reactive Resume is a free and open-source resume builder designed to help you create, manage, and share professional resumes with ease.

**Features:**
*   **No tracking or ads**: Your data is completely private.
*   **Real-time editing**: See your changes as you make them.
*   **Dozens of templates**: Choose from a variety of modern and clean designs.
*   **Custom links**: Share a personalized link to your resume.
*   **Data import**: Import your data from LinkedIn or JSON Resume.
*   **Dark mode**: A comfortable viewing experience for all.
